Help Queries

How do I make an appointment?

To make an appointment, go to the Appointment tab and select the type of appointment you would like to make. Fill out the required information and submit the form.

How do I cancel an appointment?

To cancel an appointment, go to the Appointment tab and select the appointment you would like to cancel. Click the "Cancel" button and confirm the cancellation.

What should I do if I can't make my appointment?

If you can't make your appointment, please cancel it as soon as possible so that someone else can take your spot. If you need to reschedule, you can make a new appointment.
